my wife buys flowers at the farmer's market every saturday, and i missed them while she was away on travel. when she got back she made this bouquet just the way i like it. so i painted it using a pan set and working as quickly as i could to practice for field sketching during our first vacation in italy. it's too stiff for the spirit, and the greens are too dull (should be glossier and bluer), but it was terrific fun to do.
